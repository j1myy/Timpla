# 🔥 Overview Template for Timpla (Taglish Gen‑Z Edition)

## ✨ Title

**Project Name:** Timpla ☕️  
**Type:** Web App / Ordering System / Aesthetic Showcase  
**Tool:** React + Tailwind CSS (Figma-inspired implementation) 🎨  

⸻

## 🎯 What’s This About? (The Tea 🍵)

So ayun na nga, **Timpla** is a super sosyal coffee shop website na ginawa natin to bring that "elevated local" experience straight to your screen. It’s fully responsive and functioning — from browsing that aesthetic menu to checking out your pricey (dasurv!) orders.

We decided na 'wag na mag-backend (masyado heavy, bhie), so the whole app runs on **React Context**. Yes, persistent yung cart at user session kahit i-refresh mo, kasi magic. ✨ The goal is to make it feel premium, moody, and legit elegant — yung tipong mapapa-IG story ka agad.

⸻

## 🧠 Goals & Purpose (Para San ‘To?)

*   **Vibe Check:** Establish that dark, moody, gold-accent aesthetic. Very "Titas of Manila" meets "Gen Z café hopper."
*   **Seamless Flow:** Make the ordering process smooth like butter. Add to cart -> Checkout -> Done. No stress.
*   **Showcase React Skills:** Flexing Context API for state management (auth, cart, profile) without needing a database.
*   **Local Pride:** I-highlight ang Filipino ingredients (Barako, Tablea, Ube) pero gawing high-end. 🇵🇭

⸻

## 👥 Target Users (Sino Gagamit Nito?)

*   **The "Estetik" Hunters:** Yung mga naghahanap ng bagong coffee spot for the ‘gram.
*   **Coffee Connoisseurs:** People willing to pay ₱250+ for a good cup of Dirty Horchata.
*   **Busy Bees:** Yung gusto lang umorder online nang mabilis without dealing with clunky UIs.
*   **Design-Savvy Peeps:** Users who appreciate clean typography and smooth animations (Motion library ftw!).

⸻

## 🖼 Main Features / Screens (Eto Na Nga)

*   **⭐ Home / Landing:** Intro pa lang, ulam na. Big hero image, clear philosophy, and "Featured Menu" na nakaka-budol.
*   **☕ Order Page:** The main event. Filter by Coffee, Non-Coffee, or Food. Hover effects are ✨chef's kiss✨.
*   **🛍️ Cart & Checkout:** Slide-out cart drawer (very modern) + a multi-step checkout process with progress bar. Feeling legit app talaga.
*   **🧑‍💻 Profile:** Dito makikita yung "Gold Member" status mo at yung bio mong pang-main character. May confirmation modal pa pag magla-logout kasi we care about user experience like that.

⸻

## 🚀 Prototype Flow (Paano Siya Gumagana?)

User lands on the **Home** page and gets hooked by the visuals. They click "Order Online" sa nav, tapos mapupunta sa **Menu**. Once they see the *Uji Matcha Latte* (₱330, mahal pero worth it), click "Add to Order". The cart updates instantly via Context.

Pag ready na, checkout na yarn! Fill up details -> Payment -> Success. Kung naka-login, saved ang info. Kung hindi, sign up muna, bes. Everything feels connected and snappy kasi SPA (Single Page App) siya.

⸻

## 🎨 Design Decisions (Aesthetic Check ✨)

*   **Color Scheme:** Dark mode is life (`#1a1510` background) with Gold accents (`#C6A87C`). Super classy, hindi masakit sa mata.
*   **Typography:** Serif fonts for headers (pang-magazine vibes) + Sans-serif for body text (para readable).
*   **Components:** Custom-made lahat using Tailwind v4. Rounded corners, subtle borders, at glassmorphism effects.
*   **Imagery:** Curated Unsplash photos na tugma sa color palette. Walang generic stock photos here.

⸻

## 📌 Key Takeaways (Real Talk)

This project proves na hindi mo kailangan ng complex backend to build a powerful, interactive app. With smart state management and a strong design system, Timpla delivers a premium user experience that feels just as expensive as its coffee. It’s giving **luxury**, it’s giving **local love**, it’s giving **deploy na ‘to!** 🚀
