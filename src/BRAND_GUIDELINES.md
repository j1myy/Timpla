# ☕️ Timpla: Brand Identity & Guidelines

## 🌟 Brand Essence
**Name:** Timpla  
**Tagline:** *Elevated Local. Unapologetically Premium.*  
**Vibe:** "Sosyal yet Local" / "Old Manila Nostalgia meets Modern Luxury"

⸻

## 📖 The Story (Ang Kwento)
Timpla isn't just a coffee shop; it's a statement. We noticed a gap: you have your neighborhood *kapeng barako* stands (authentic but casual) and your international coffee chains (premium but generic).

**Timpla** bridges that gap. We take the humble Filipino ingredients—Barako, Tablea, Ube, Pandan—and give them the luxury treatment they deserve. We serve history in a ceramic cup. We don't just brew coffee; we curate an experience for the modern Filipino who loves their heritage but *lives* for the aesthetic.

⸻

## 🧬 Brand DNA

### 💎 Core Values
1.  **Heritage High-End:** We don't cheap out on local. We prove that *local* can be the most expensive item on the menu.
2.  **Visuals First:** If it's not Instagrammable, we don't serve it. The presentation is half the flavor.
3.  **Slow Living:** We are the antidote to "fast coffee." Our Cold Brew steeps for 24 hours. Our pours are deliberate.

### 🗣️ Brand Voice
*   **Sophisticated but Warm:** Think of a rich Tita who loves storytelling.
*   **Confident:** We know our price points are high (₱250++), and we don't apologize for it because the quality is unmatched.
*   **Cultural:** We use terms like *Batirol*, *Timpla*, and *Kape* with pride, mixing them with modern café terminology.

⸻

## 🎨 Visual Identity

### 🎨 Color Palette
*   **Espresso Noir (`#1a1510`):** The primary background. Deep, dark, and mysterious.
*   **Antique Gold (`#C6A87C`):** The accent. Used for buttons, borders, and highlights. Represents luxury and the perfect crema.
*   **Ceramic Cream (`#E8E0D5`):** Text color. Softer than pure white, easier on the eyes, feels like old paper or stoneware.

### ✒️ Typography
*   **Headlines (Serif):** Elegant, editorial, traditional. Used for impact.
*   **Body (Sans-Serif):** Clean, minimal, readable. Used for the details.

⸻

## 🏆 Signature Offerings (The "Budol" List)

Our menu is curated to justify the price tag. We don't sell "coffee"; we sell "liquid gold."

*   **Barako Gold Espresso (₱250):** Not your lolo's barako. Refined, intense, single-origin.
*   **Dirty Horchata (₱300):** A fusion favorite. Rice milk meets caffeine.
*   **Bibingka Galette (₱380):** A French pastry technique applied to a Filipino Christmas staple.
